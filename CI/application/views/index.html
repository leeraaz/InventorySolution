Tee is used to join three pipes at the same point. It acts like a junction for the pipe which helps water to redirect them to left and right. It is also used in taps for supply of hot and cold water.

Socket is used to join two pipes. It acts like an extender which connect two different pipes and matain flow of water in the same direction.

Union is as much as Socket but it is used after the completion of project where pipe is need to be cut for some repair and joins them back after completing repair.
