CPVC Pipe and Fittings

With the introduction of improved technology in the area of piping system Panchakanya Group established another Unplasticized Chlorinated Polyvinyl Chloride (CPVC) Pipe manufacturing unit to produce odorless, non-toxic, non-contaminating and hygienic pipe with high merits and cost economy.

 This pipe is regarded as the most ideal for water distribution system in agriculture. Today the PVC pipes are mostly preferred im plumbing works, because of light-weight and easiness in handling for the use in high rise buildings. Its appearance is better than traditional MS and C.I. pipes.


Advantages - Light weight
- Resistance to chemical, electrolytic & galvanized corrosion
- Self Extinguishing.
- Smoothness of Surface & Excellent Flow Characteristics
- Excellent Tensile and High Impact Strength
- Easy to Install and long lasting
